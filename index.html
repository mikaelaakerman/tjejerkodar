Whistleblower

Whistleblower is a non-profit effort that strives to ensure a free, open and transparent society where everyone is prepared to blow the whistle against wrongdoings and corruption.

We create a safe environment where people can speak up, help lay the foundation for democracy and be a part of the future of journalism.

You carry the story – we just help you share it with the world.

Join our cause by donating or signing up for our newsletter. No spam – just knowledge and empowerment.

About us

Whistleblower was founded in 2016 by Swedish journalist Mikaela Akerman with financial support from Google and the Swedish Journalist Union.

Mikaela is a former news reporter at the Swedish Daily. She now works as a Media Industry Editor at Omni, developing new products for Schibsted Media.

She has a degree in science journalism and has also worked as a freelance correspondent, covering science, tech and politics from Los Angeles and Silicon Valley.
